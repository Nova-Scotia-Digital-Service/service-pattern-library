Service Design System - Patterns

Phase Banner examples

Use the phase banner component to show users your service is still being worked on.

When to use this component

Use the phase banner component if your service is still being developed. Use an alpha banner when your service is in alpha, and a beta banner if your service is in private or public beta.

How it works

Your banner must be directly under the blue NS mothership ribbon header.

Add a feedback link

Use a ‘feedback’ link to collect on-page feedback about your service. This can open an email or take the user to a dedicated page or form. Whatever option you use, make sure that users do not lose their place in the service and can return to the page they were on.

When collecting feedback about a service via a form, be sure that you're not collecting any personally identifiable information as part of the feedback. There should be no way to link the feedback provided to any of the information a user enters into a service.

Alpha Banner

Use an Alpha banner when you are still trying out new things and the service is not fully functional.

Alpha This is a new service that we're still developing. Try the service and send us your comments and suggestions. Your feedback will help make the service easier to use for everyone.

Beta Banner

Use a Beta banner when you service is working and close to going live (private beta), or has recently gone live (beta) and is activley being inproved based on user data and feedback.

Beta This is a new service that we're working on improving. Use the service and send us your comments and suggestions. Your feedback will help make the service easier to use for everyone.
