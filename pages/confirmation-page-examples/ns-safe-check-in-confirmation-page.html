What to do next

Self-isolate

Continue to self-isolate as directed by Public Health.

Check-in daily during your self-isolation

You will receive a daily email reminder with a link to complete your check-in online. It’s important that you complete your online check-in every day so Public Health can monitor your health and support you while your self-isolating.

Monitor your health

It’s important to continue to self-isolate and monitor your health. If you have any new or worsening COVID-19 symptoms, include those details during your daily check-in. Public Health will contact you as soon as possible to provide information on what to do next. If you require urgent medical attention, contact 811 or 911.

Download COVID Alert

Protect yourself and others with COVID Alert. COVID Alert is a free exposure notification app that lets you know if you may have been exposed to COVID-19. You can also use the app to let others know if you test positive without sharing any personal information.
